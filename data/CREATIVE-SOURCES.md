## 1. CORE ANIMATION & 3D LIBRARIES (Official Repos with Examples)

### 1.1 Three.js (Official)
- **URL**: https://github.com/mrdoob/three.js
- **Stars**: ~110k
- **Examples Dir**: https://github.com/mrdoob/three.js/tree/dev/examples
- **Techniques**: Full 3D rendering, shaders, post-processing, physics, particles, WebGPU
- **Quality**: 5/5 - THE definitive 3D web library, hundreds of production examples
- **License**: MIT
- **Training Value**: The /examples directory alone contains 300+ demos covering every WebGL/WebGPU technique

### 1.2 GSAP (GreenSock Animation Platform)
- **URL**: https://github.com/greensock/GSAP
- **Stars**: ~24k
- **Techniques**: Timeline animations, ScrollTrigger, morphSVG, DrawSVG, SplitText, Flip
- **Quality**: 5/5 - Industry standard for web animation, used by every top agency
- **License**: Standard GreenSock License (free for most uses)
- **Training Value**: ScrollTrigger examples are essential for modern scroll-driven sites

### 1.3 Lenis (by Darkroom Engineering, formerly Studio Freight)

- **Quality**: 5/5 - Used by nearly every Awwwards-winning site since 2023
- **License**: MIT
- **Training Value**: Foundational for the "feel" of premium websites

### 1.4 Barba.js

- **Quality**: 5/5 - The standard for SPA-like transitions in multi-page sites
- **License**: MIT
- **Training Value**: Critical for understanding fluid page-to-page navigation

### 1.5 Theatre.js

- **License**: Apache 2.0 (core) / AGPL 3.0 (studio)

### 1.6 Motion (formerly Framer Motion)

### 1.7 Anime.js
